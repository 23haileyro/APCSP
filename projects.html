Projects

In my art class this year, we were assigned to alter a book
(cutting pages, painting over pages, and creating something new).
I decided to create a pirate ship using paper mache, use a glue formula
to make the pages (waves) stick upwards and acrylic paint. Through
this process, I learned how to manipulate different mediums to create
what I want and also try something new.


For my AP Environmental Science class, I researched and did
a case study on dead zones and their affects on Gulf of Mexico
(as well as the surrounding communities that rely on the Gulf for
the economy). By completing this project, I learned more about the way
the world works and the importance of the Gulf. Also, I learned more
about the species in the Gulf, the ways people rely on the Gulf, and the way the Gulf changes negatively.
